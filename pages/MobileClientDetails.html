Q: What is FATCA and why are we collection these details?

FATCA stands for the Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act. It is a new piece of legislation to help counter tax evasion in the US. Introduced by the United States Department of Treasury (Treasury) and the US Internal Revenue Service (IRS), the purpose of FATCA is to encourage better tax compliance by preventing US persons from using banks, Mutual Funds and other financial organizations to avoid US taxation on their income and assets.
We are collecting FATCA information from you, on behalf of the mutual funds where you invest, to confirm the FATCA status and where necessary we may have to contact you for further information and documentation.
